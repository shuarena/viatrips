Tour

What are the conditions for registering for a tour?

To register for a tour, customers need to prepare their Identity Card (still valid for use) for domestic tours, and Passport (still valid for over 6 months) for overseas tours. Moreover, for certain countries, customers will also need to prepare additional documents to apply for a visa, as required by the Embassy/Consulate regulations.

How long in advance do I need to register for a tour? How long in advance do I need to prepare the documents?

Dear customer, to ensure a good seat and a well-prepared itinerary, you should register at least 1 month in advance compared to the departure date. Specifically for tours that require a visa application, you will need to prepare documents to apply for a visa according to the specific requirements of each destination country.

What are the conditions for elderly customers to join a tour? Can I book a tour directly for an elderly person without any conditions?

For female customers from 55 years old and male customers from 60 years old: Vietravel advises that it is best to have a relative who is sufficiently healthy (and under 55 years old) accompany them to be able to provide timely assistance if an issue occurs during the tour. Especially for customers from 70 years old and above: it is mandatory to have a relative under 55 years old accompany them and submit a health check certificate, which includes a doctor's confirmation of fitness to travel + Health Commitment Form according to the company's template.

What are the conditions for customers under 18 to register for a tour?

Guests under 18 years old must be accompanied by their parents or a relative over 18 years old. In the case where the guest under 18 is traveling with a relative, the parents must provide a power of attorney (authorized by local authorities) for the relative.

Do I have to pay in full or can I make a deposit?

+ In the case of purchasing online through the website www.viatrips.com and choosing the online payment method, please pay 100% of the tour value as specified in the booking confirmation.
+ In the case of direct payment or bank transfer, you can pay a deposit of 50% of the tour value, and the remaining balance must be paid 7 - 10 days before the tour starts for normal days and 10-15 days before for holidays or Tet (Lunar New Year).

Where can I make a cash payment? Do I need to visit the company's office?

For cash or bank card payments, customers can make payments at the head office located at: 302 Nguyen Van Dau street, district Binh Thanh, HCMC.

Can the company make payments using a bank card? Are there any fees for using the card?

Customers can use their card to make payments at the POS systems at Viatrips offices; however, the cardholder must be present. Viatrips will assist with the card payment fees. For international cards or credit cards used for payments in foreign currencies, Viatrips will also assist, but the customer will bear the currency conversion fees to Vietnamese Dong (VND), which depend on the bank and the timing of the transaction.

Is the tour price inclusive of VAT? Can I request an invoice?

All tours include VAT. In the case that a customer requests an invoice, please provide complete billing information immediately upon registering for the tour and completing payment. The invoice will be issued after the tour booking is finalized and will be sent to the customer's address as per request or collected directly. Invoices will not be issued after the tour has commenced.

Group Booking

How do I start a group booking?

Initiate a group booking by selecting "Create Group" on the homepage. You will receive an invitation link, which you can share with other members to join the group.

How can group members enter their personal information?

Each group member can access the shared group link and enter their personal information in the "Personal Information" section.

How do we share and pay individual bills?

When a booking is made, the system automatically calculates and shares the cost among each member. Each member will receive a notification about the amount to be paid and can make the payment online through supported payment methods.

Is this feature safe and does it protect personal information?

We are committed to the confidentiality of personal information. All personal data is used solely for booking purposes and is not shared with third parties.

What if a member wants to leave the group?

Members can leave the group at any time. However, if the booking has been confirmed, cancellation or changes may be subject to the cancellation policy of the booked service.

Can booking information be changed after completion?

Changes to booking information depend on the policy of each service and may incur change fees. It is recommended to contact the service provider directly for modifications.

Planning

How do I start planning my trip on the website?

Begin by creating an account and using the "Trip Planning" feature. Enter information about your destination, travel dates, and personal preferences, and the system will suggest a suitable itinerary.

Can the website suggest destinations for me?

Yes, based on the information and preferences you provide, the system will recommend suitable destinations and activities for you.

How do I receive reminders about my itinerary?

You will automatically receive itinerary reminders via email and push notifications on your phone if you have installed our mobile app.

Can I change my travel plans after they have been scheduled?

Yes, you can change your travel plans at any time through the website.

How do I cancel or modify my travel plans?

You can cancel or modify your travel plans through the "My Plans" section on the website or app.
