Day 1:

The tour starts in Ho Chi Minh City with a flight to Da Nang. Visitors will be picked up at Da Nang airport and travel to Hue. Along the way, they'll stop at places like Lập An Lagoon, Thái Hà Camphor Essential Oil, Thiên Mụ Pagoda, and the Imperial City. They'll also experience Hue's nightlife.

Day 2:

The journey continues to Quang Binh to explore Phong Nha Cave and Thiên Đường Cave, both part of the Phong Nha - Kẻ Bàng UNESCO World Heritage Site. After the tour, visitors have free time to rest at the hotel and have the opportunity to explore Quang Binh at night."

Day 3:

The itinerary includes visits to historical sites in Quảng Trị, La Vang Sanctuary, and a stop at Lucky Pearl. The day ends with a visit to Sơn Trà Peninsula and the Linh Ứng Pagoda. In the evening, guests can explore the beautiful night scenes of Da Nang.

Day 4:

Travelers visit Đà Nẵng's Mỹ Khê Beach, Ba Na Hills for sightseeing and entertainment. Guests self-entertain and have lunch at Ba Na Hills. In the afternoon and evening, guests visit Hội An's ancient town for cultural exploration and lantern floating on the Hoai River.

Day 5:

The last day involves shopping for local specialties and a return trip to Ho Chi Minh City from Đà Nẵng, and say goodbye to our beautiful trip!

• What's the best time to visit Da Nang?

The best time is during summer, specifically from early April to the end of August in the Gregorian calendar.

• What special dishes can I try in Da Nang?

Famous dishes include grilled pork wrapped in rice paper, Cau Mong roasted beef, ...

• When can I see the Dragon Bridge spouting fire?

The Dragon Bridge spouts fire and water at 9:00 PM every Saturday and Sunday.

• Can a child under 2 years old join the tour to Cu Lao Cham?

Yes, the child and family can enjoy beach time at Cu Lao Cham.

• Do infants need to pay when visiting Ba Na Hills?

Tickets, including cable car rides, are priced based on the child's height. Under 1m: free, from 1m-1.4m: child fare: 600,000 VND/child; above 1.4m: adult fare: 800,000 VND (prices as of 31/12/2020).

• If my family doesn’t visit Ba Na Hills, what can we do during the tour's Ba Na Hills visit?

Your family can independently explore attractions around Da Nang: Love Lock Bridge, Asia Park, Con Market, and more.

• Can elderly individuals join the tour to visit Paradise Cave?

Yes, if they are in good health condition, as Paradise Cave is dry and during the tour, there's a 1km walk on wooden stairs within the cave.

• When is the best time to visit Cu Lao Cham?

Cu Lao Cham is most beautiful typically from early April to the end of August in the Gregorian calendar every year.

• What's the usual star rating for hotels in Da Nang?

Tours typically stay at 4-star standard hotels.

• Can bring outside food to Ba Na Hills??

As Ba Na Hills offers numerous restaurants and food stalls, visitors are not allowed to bring outside food to ensure food safety and hygiene within the tourist area.
